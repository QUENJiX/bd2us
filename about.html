About Bangladeshis at Bideshi Universities (BABU)

Our Vision

We see a future where every ambitious Bangladeshi student, no matter how big their dreams or how confusing the path seems, feels empowered and equipped to successfully navigate the U.S. college application journey.

We aim to be the clear, comprehensive, and supportive guide that turns the "super duper ultra confusing" process into a manageable path towards achieving their higher education goals in the United States.
