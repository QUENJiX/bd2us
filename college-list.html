The College List: Your Strategic Battle Plan

This is not a list of "good schools." This is a strategic breakdown of where you might actually have a chance of getting funded. Forget the rankings you see on Instagram. The only ranking that matters is how generous a school is to a high-need international student from Bangladesh. I've grouped them into tiers of reality. Don't waste your time applying to 20 schools from the "God Tier" unless you have a Nobel Prize waiting for you. Be smart. Be strategic.

Disclaimer: All data (SAT scores, deadlines, aid policies) changes annually. These are estimates from the 2024-2025 cycle. YOU MUST VERIFY EVERY SINGLE DATA POINT ON THE COLLEGE'S OFFICIAL WEBSITE. Trusting this list blindly is academic suicide.
